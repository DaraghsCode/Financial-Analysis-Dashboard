# Financial Performance Analysis

## Business Problem
A company provides you with a profit & loss statement for FY 23,24, & 25.<br>
It is your role to analyse the firms performance and provide recommendations going forward based on your findings.
## Dataset
A basic self generated P&L statement in the form of a csv file (sample_pnl_statement.csv).
## Tools Used
Python (pandas): Data collection, cleanup, exploration, basic visualisation.<br>
Tableau: Data visualisation for the user/client.<br>
Using Python I would read in the raw data and operate on it.<br>
When the analysis was done I exported the processed data into a new csv that fed my Tableau file.
## Key Insights
1. Revenue & margin growth is steady and seasonal, consistent boost from Q2-Q3
2. Cost of Goods Sold remains the biggest cost driver by a significant margin
3. Marketing costs are rising faster than revenue (35% from 2023-2025 compared to 33%)
4. Cost of goods sold remain steady as revenue grows
5. Operating expenses and Salaries are decreasing as a percentage of revenue - efficiencies developing

## Business Recommendations
1. Review marketing ROI and channel effectiveness due to marketing spend growing faster than revenue.
2. Continue operational efficiency initiatives, as operating expenses and salaries are declining relative to revenue growth.
3. Closely monitor profit margins as the business scales to ensure expense growth remains sustainable.
4. Align inventory and resource planning with recurring seasonal demand trends, particularly during stronger Q2–Q3 periods.
5. Explore opportunities to reduce COGS through supplier negotiations, bulk purchasing, or supply chain optimization, as COGS remain the largest expense driver.
